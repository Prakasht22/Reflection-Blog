Welcome To My First Blog

Introduction

My Learning outcome from “What is Information”       This unit mainly tells about ‘What is Information’ and many ways of understanding information. Particularly explained through the Data - Information - Knowledge - Wisdom model. Before learning this chapter I assumed that information is just some data which is used as some kind of evidence. But this unit helped me to understand the deeper outcomes of information.  While learning this unit I found interest in the DIKW (Data - Information - Knowledge - Wisdom ) model which is new to me . Because it tells us that models aren’t perfect, we can change according to information and we can improve better. And the DIKW model made me think critically. The DIKW model helped me to understand that human thinking has some limits, especially when we deal with a lot of information at once. I realized that our decisions are not perfect every time because our memory can only handle some data. This shows why we cannot always defend only on our own thinking. It also helped me to understand that mathematical and computational tools are important because they help us, to improve accuracy, and help us make better decisions. This unit helped me to change my opinion on information and how to use it. After completing this unit I learned that information is not just the  simple data, it is the data organised and presented in a useful way, but something that can lead to knowledge and better decisions. The DIKW model helped me think more deeply and critically. I also understood that human thinking has limits, so we need support from tools and technology. Overall, this unit has improved my thinking skills and will help me in my future studies and real-life decision-making.
