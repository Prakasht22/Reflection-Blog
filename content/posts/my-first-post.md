I realized that our decisions are not perfect every time because our memory can only handle some data. This shows why we cannot always defend only on our own thinking. It also helped me to understand that mathematical and computational tools are important because they help us, to improve accuracy, and help us make better decisions. This unit helped me to change my opinion on information and how to use it.
